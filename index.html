How to Play

Guess the drug name in 6 tries. Each round randomly asks for either the generic or brand name — check the badge at the top of the grid so you know which one to guess. Type letters and press Enter. Spaces are filled in automatically.

Tile Colors

A
Letter is in the correct position
A
Letter is in the word but wrong position
A
Letter is not in the word

Hints Unlock Progressively

Drug class is shown immediately. Specific class unlocks after guess 2. Brand name unlocks after guess 3. Controlled status is always shown.
